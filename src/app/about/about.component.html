About Us

Welcome to Jai Exports India

At Jai Exports India, we believe that everyday choices can lead to a cleaner, greener planet. That’s why we’re dedicated to providing high-quality Bagasse plates — made from sugarcane residue, a renewable and sustainable resource.

Our plates are 100% biodegradable, compostable, and completely free from plastic and harmful chemicals. Whether you're hosting an event, running a food business, or simply seeking eco-conscious alternatives, our products provide a safe, sturdy, and sustainable solution.

At Jai Exports India, we’re a purpose-driven team passionate about redefining everyday dining. Using sugarcane bagasse — a renewable by-product of sugarcane processing — we craft premium tableware that replaces plastic and conserves forests.
